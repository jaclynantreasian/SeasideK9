Frequently Asked Questions

How early can I drop off, and how late can I pick up?

Earliest dropoff time is 6am. Last pickup is 9pm. Any pickups after 9pm will be charged an additional $15.

Can I come by before a stay, for a meet and greet?

Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, we are currently only conducting meet and greets via phone or video calls.

What do I need to pack for my dog’s stay?

Pack lightly! We have plenty of beds, bowls, toys, blankets, and crates available if your dog prefers to sleep in one. This ensures nothing of yours is lost or ruined. We prefer if your dog’s food is individually bagged out for each meal, or each day of their stay. 50 pound bags and plastic containers of food can become cumbersome for us to store and handle.

What do you need from me before a stay?

We will need a digital copy of your dog’s vaccination records. We check for current rabies, bordetella, and distemper vaccinations. This ensures the health and safety of all pups in our care!
