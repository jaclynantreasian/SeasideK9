Let's play all day!

Show your pet how much you care by giving them a loving and fun home away from home. Our dogs spend fun-filled days romping around our spacious backyard, make field trips to scenic South Shore beaches and trails and come back to our home in Quincy to cuddle with one of our human or canine caretakers on our comfortable couches. We believe dogs need lots of enrichment and make sure to incorporate structured walks, socialization, and food puzzles into all stays. Since our doggy daycare is small and personal, we keep our pack sizes small so your dog can be showered in care and attention all day long!

A safe and loving home away from home

    Your dog deserves the best care in their home away from home, that's why we make the comfort and safety of our dogs our first priority. Some of the special health and safety considerations we take for your pet include:

  • We want to reduce infectious disease transmission in our facility as much as possible so we have stepped up our rigorous cleaning protocols with a top-of-the-line HVAC system and UV disinfecting light in our space.
  • Natural grass has been replaced by hygienic turf for safe and clean romps in our spacious backyard.
  • All of our pack leaders are certified in animal first aid and CPR, as well as have experience in medication administration.
  • Our team has over 15 years of combined experienced in pups of all breeds, ages and special needs. You can trust us to care for your pet.
